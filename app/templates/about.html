Travel Buddy

The purpose of the Travel Buddy web application is to connect people who want to travel around Europe together and provide them with a checklist of countries they have previously visited or countries that they want to visit. Users of this web application can see who has already visited the country that they personally want to visit, and send them an email with questions about their destination.

To use the application, go to the map section and choose a country of interest. When registered and logged in, you can add a country to your bucket list and see who also wants to visit the same country!
